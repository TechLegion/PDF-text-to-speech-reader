Help

Need assistance with our PDF to Text Speech Converter? Here are some frequently asked questions:

How do I use the converter?

To convert a PDF to text and speech, simply navigate to the Upload page, select your PDF file, and click the convert button.

What file formats are supported?

We currently support PDF files for conversion into text and speech.

Can I customize the speech settings?

Yes, you can adjust the speech parameters such as language, voice type, and speed on the conversion page.
