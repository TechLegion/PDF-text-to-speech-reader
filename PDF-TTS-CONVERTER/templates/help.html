Go to the Upload page, select your PDF file, choose your preferred language, and click Upload & Convert. After processing, you can play or download the generated MP3 audio.

Only PDF files are supported for upload and conversion.

Yes! If your PDF is image-based (scanned), the app will automatically use OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract text. This may take a bit longer than regular text-based PDFs.

You can choose from a variety of languages and accents (English, French, Spanish, Mandarin, Dutch, Portuguese, Japanese, German, Russian, Nepali, Korean, Italian, Ukrainian, Arabic) on the upload page.

If no text can be extracted (even after OCR), you'll see an error message and no audio will be generated. Try another PDF or check that your file contains readable text.

Uploaded files are stored temporarily for processing and audio generation. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
