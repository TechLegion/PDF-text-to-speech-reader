About PDF to Speech Converter

Empowering accessibility and productivity for everyone, everywhere.

Our Mission

PDF to Speech Converter was created by TechLegion to make information more accessible and usable for all. Whether you have visual impairments, prefer listening, or just want to multitask, our tool is here to help.

What We Offer

  • Effortless Upload: Instantly convert your PDF documents to high-quality audio.
  • Multi-Language Support: Choose from a variety of languages and accents.
  • OCR Technology: Even scanned/image-based PDFs are supported with automatic text recognition.
  • Download & Listen: Play your audio online or download it for offline use.
  • Modern, User-Friendly Design: Enjoy a seamless, intuitive experience on any device.

How It Works

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Select your preferred language.
  3. Let our system extract and convert the text to speech.
  4. Listen online or download your MP3 audio file.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fast, reliable, and secure conversion.
  • No hidden fees or ads.
  • Built with care by TechLegion for real-world accessibility and productivity.
